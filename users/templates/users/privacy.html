Privacy Policy

LexiPark annotates Korean text on pages you visit and lets you save words to your personal vocabulary.

Data We Collect
  • Authentication information: tokens to keep you signed in.
  • Website content: page text may be processed to identify words and, when you request it, minimal text segments may be sent to our server for analysis or translation.
  • User activity: clicks on annotated words to save them to your vocabulary.
What We Do Not Collect

We do not collect personal communications, financial, health, precise location, or complete web browsing history.

Use of Data

Data is used only to provide annotation, translation, and vocabulary features. We do not sell data or share it with third parties.

Storage & Retention

Authentication tokens and cached translations are stored in your browser. Saved vocabulary is stored in your LexiPark account. You can clear browser storage or delete your account to remove data.

Security

We use HTTPS and industry-standard controls. No remote code is executed by the extension.

Children

Not directed to children under 13.
